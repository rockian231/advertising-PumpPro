About Us

PUMP PRO CONCRETE MACHINERY CORPORATION was established in 1974 and it is the sole manufacturer in Taiwan which makes professional concrete pump tuck. In the few decades, PUMP PRO CONCRETE MACHINERY has been working on research development, improvement for a better series concrete pump truck and excellent performance, and easily operation. PUMP PRO CONCRETE MACHINERY was awarded from many countries like Germany, China, and Japan etc. Concrete pump trucks can be widely used in any of the civic construction engineering such as building, tunnel, bridge, runway of airport, wharf, highway, and dam, etc. PUMP PRO CONCRETE MACHINERY plays an important role in the area of national major construction sector, construction companies, and concrete blenders.

Products and Services

Pump Pro Concret Machinery is the exclusive distributor of Chen Fong Concrete Truck brand in the Philippines, with the complete parts and accessories imported from taiwan.

This company is also catering services on concrete pump rentals for construction projects in various sites or areas within Metro Manila and nearby cities, Cavite, Laguna, Bulacan and other places.

Rendering services on pumpcrete truck rentals in pouring ready-mix concrete for construction of high-rise buildings up to 100th floor, residentials houses, condominium, commercial buildings, (such as malls, markets, hotels, etc).

CHEN FONG Concrete Pump Truck's characteristic:

CHEN FONG Concrete Pump Truck's characteristic:

1.CF pump vale with wolfram steel: It is made by wolfram with special extra work to get the good quality and wonderful functions, and it is awarded from Germany, China and Japan...etc.


2.The design of the main oil tub: It divides into oil and water. When the oil seal got break, the tub will separate the oil and water directly.


3.Injection lubrication system for pump valve shaft and reamer shaft adopts automatic instant-volume control which decreased the waste of grease.


4.Hydraulic cooling system: With larger output, and smaller back force, the end-pipe operation can be easily operated. The heat Ventilation System equipped with water cooling type and air-cooling type which can get rid of heat more efficiently.


5.The mixing blade can change its direction automatically while it meets the huge resistance.


6.The machine is equipped with the lower speed function in case that the concrete being pumped up and got stock in the pipeline.


7.Maintenance and after-sales service: The company is Taiwan's largest concrete pump truck professional factory and maintenance plant. CF can provide the customers the most convenient and efficient maintenance and after-sales service immediately.
